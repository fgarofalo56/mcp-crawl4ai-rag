## Approach Options
- `--approach=iterative`: Iterative development with feedback loops (default)
- `--approach=waterfall`: Sequential phase completion

## Validation Levels
- `--validation-level=basic`: Essential validations only
- `--validation-level=comprehensive`: Full validation suite (default)
